ABOUT FIAA


FIAA is a user-friendly website that gathers stock information from Yahoo Finance, analyzes stocks, and provides better financial stock reports using artificial intelligence and machine learning algorithms. Our goal is to help new stock traders reduce investment risks by giving them suggestions on when to buy or sell a stock and how to invest in the long-term and short-term. Moreover, we have been implementing a new feature that does not exist on other platforms: stock prediction.

However, it is essential to keep in mind that accurate stock prediction is extremely challenging because we cannot anticipate all changes over time. Therefore, the stock prediction feature is not always 100% accurate.
